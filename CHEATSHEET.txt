## Enchantments

Add 3 enchantments that can be applied to laser blades using enchanting table.

### Laser Blade

Increases attack damage and attack speed of the weapon.

Enchantment Recipe

    Laser Blade Blueprint
        Any laser blade + Glowstone Dust + Block of Diamond + Glowstone Dust

### Light Element

Increases damage against undead mobs and illagers.

Enchantment Recipe

    Laser Blade Blueprint
        Any laser blade + Glowstone + Diamond + Glowstone

### Repulsive Force

Allows Laser Blades to block attacks and increases knockback strength at higher levels.
Repulsive Force is provided by an independent built-in data pack and can therefore be disabled separately.

Enchantment Recipe

    Laser Blade Blueprint
        Any laser blade + Iron Ingot + Diamond + Block of Gold
